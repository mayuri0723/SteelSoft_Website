Services

What We Offer

Architectural Design

An initial step an engineer will take is to review the drawings produced by the architect/building designer. We at SteelSoft will look at various types of units, its location and framing, type of accommodation, type of support systems available. This information will be referred back to the architect to make any necessary adjustments if required.

Structural Design

The engineer in-charge will determine site specific requirements for the structure that are dependent on its location.Once the environmental factors have been determined, the engineer will perform an initial design of building elements such as roof, wall and floor layouts per the architectural drawings, footings and/or slabs, bearing walls including beams and columns, and identify potential mechanical, electrical, and plumbing conflicts.

MEP Drafting & Services

SteelSoft renders expert engineering design consultancy services for HVAC, Plumbing, Fire Protection, Electricals, Security, Public Address, Building Management System and much more, for Residential, Township, High Rise, Commercial, IT Park, Hotels, Hospitals, Malls & Multiplexes, Institutional & Industrial sectors. The Company is capable of managing projects of any scale and complexity.

Project Management

We provide Project Management consultancy services for Design and Construction which includes designs support, planning, monitoring, quality checking, tracking progress of execution on day-to-day basis by deploying permanent man power at site.

Project Consultation

We provide Peer Review, Proof consultancy and value engineering services for design and construction. This includes checking the existing concept design, detail designs, bill of quantities, technical and commercial specification etc. and suggesting means for optimization of designs.

HR Fabrication

Our top priority is to provide the right information to ease fabrication as well as erection. Our Structural Steel Detailing Services includes preparing the accurate and complete fabrication drawings for all structural steel components as per Structural Design Drawings. These components commonly include; Columns, Beams, Braces, Frames, Girts, Purlins etc. We have our own fabrication team.

Feasibility Study

Getting the project to a company is the basic and the pre requirement for which everyone strives to achieve in this competitive market. Hence accurate pre-study analysis of a given project in terms of steel quantity plays a key role for achieving a project. Having global experience of more than +20yrs, we at SteelSoft help to give the close estimate for a steel quantity by carrying out the feasibility study for a given project.

BIM Modeling

Our teams provide end-to-end Building Information Modeling (BIM) services right from 2D & 3D CAD drafting designs to BIM implementation and consulting with an expertise in Virtual Designs and Construction (VDC) technology. Our consultants build partnership with architects, engineers, contractors, surveyors and manufacturing firms to gauge BIM methodologies.

Project Costing & Estimation

We provide quantity estimation for all types of Real estate, Infrastructure, Industrial and commercial projects for our esteemed clients in India as well as abroad. We provide estimation for different stages of constructions like conceptual pre-launch, detailed pre-contract / tender estimate, post-contract/GFC estimate based on the client objectives.

CFS – Operation Sequence

Enquiry from Client

Once received the drawings from Client, Our Engineering team do feasibility study for possibility of CFS structure and approx. BOM for costing purpose.

Design & Engineering

Our Engineering team do analysis and design of CFS structure using artificial intelligence software like Framecad,Revit, Staad etc for Value engineering

Modelling

Our BIM Modelling team generates 3D visual interface to integrate MEP and all allied services. The final model includes actual BOM, PMC, Construction sequence and shop drawing for manufacturing.

Manufacturing & Logistics

Assembly & Erection
